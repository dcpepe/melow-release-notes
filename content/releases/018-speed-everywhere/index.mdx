We rewrote the query execution engine from the ground up. Average response times dropped from 6 seconds to under 2. Complex joins across multiple data sources feel instant now.

AI Brain now indexes your data sources in parallel instead of sequentially. A full re-index that used to take 4 hours now completes in under 45 minutes.
